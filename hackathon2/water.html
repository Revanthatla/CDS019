Water Safety Tips for Kids

1. Always Swim with an Adult

Never go swimming alone! Make sure an adult is watching you at all times.

2. Wear a Life Jacket

If you're not a strong swimmer, wear a life jacket to stay safe in the water.

3. Stay Away from Deep Water

Don't go into deep water unless you're sure it's safe and you're with an adult.
