Learn all about traffic lights and road safety to stay safe on the streets!

Traffic Lights Explained

Red Light

Stop! Vehicles must stop until the light turns green.

Yellow Light

Slow Down! Get ready to stop.

Green Light

Go! You can move through the intersection safely.

Important Road Signs

  • Stop Sign: You must stop and look carefully.
  • School Zone: Drive slowly near schools.
  • Crosswalk: Watch for pedestrians crossing the road.
  • Speed Limit: Do not exceed the posted speed limit.

Safety Tips for Kids

  • Always look both ways before crossing the street.
  • Use crosswalks or pedestrian signals.
  • Wear a helmet when riding a bicycle.
  • Never run across the street without checking for vehicles.
