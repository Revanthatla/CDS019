Stay Safe from Strangers!

Everything you need to know about staying safe around strangers.

What is a Stranger?

A stranger is anyone you don’t know well. They can be friendly, but you should always be cautious.

How to Stay Safe

  • Don’t accept gifts from strangers.
  • Never go anywhere with someone you don’t know.
  • Stay in safe, public places.
  • Use a buddy system and stay with friends.

What to Do if You Feel Unsafe

If you ever feel unsure or unsafe, find a trusted adult like a parent, teacher, or police officer.


How to Identify a Stranger

They might not always look scary. Even someone who looks friendly is a stranger if you don’t know them.

If someone tries to give you something or asks you to come with them, that’s a red flag!


Remember: It's okay to say NO to a stranger and find help from a trusted adult!
