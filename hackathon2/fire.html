Fire Safety for Kids

Learn how to stay safe in case of a fire!

Precautions

  • Always keep matches and lighters away from children.
  • Check smoke alarms monthly.
  • Know two ways out of every room.
  • Practice your fire escape plan with your family.

Actions to Take During a Fire

  • Stay calm and get low to the ground.
  • Feel the door with the back of your hand before opening it.
